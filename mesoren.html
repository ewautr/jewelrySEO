Hello, I am Søren Kølbæk.

I'm 19 years old, currently living in Copenhagen, Denmark. I make jewelry, I love skateboarding and I walk a lot. I have lots of ambitions, drive and sense of design. I think very versatile and I am good at adapting my abilities to the situation. My interest is and always has been jewelry and goldsmith art in various shapes, sizes and styles.

 

I make my jewellery yours.

I would love to meet you before I sell you any of my pieces. Knowing what you need and what my jewelry means to you makes me more creative and enhusiastic. You can either choose one of my projects or we can create something unique together. Feel free to contact me and tell me about your idea, I'm sure we will figure something out.
