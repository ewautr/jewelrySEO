how i work

my process of creating:

Design.

I usually design the jewelry in my sketchbook anytime something comes to my mind. But these are just raw sketches I later on moderate in my workshop.

Materials.

I create handmade pieces mostly in silver and copper. Some of my projects are made of brass but I'm willing to personalize them in any material you choose.

Custom.

I can customize the designs I already created the jewelry of your dreams or you can present me your idea for a piece and we will figure it out together.
